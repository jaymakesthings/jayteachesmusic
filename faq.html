Q: How long are your lessons?

A: Great Q! My lessons will typically vary in length from 30 to 60 minutes, depending on what the student is most comfortable with. If requested, I would also be more than happy to teach lessons greater than one hour in length!

Q: What are your rates?

A: Wow, another great question! For lessons taught in your home, an hour-long session is $60 and a half-hour is $40. If the student's preference is to take lessons via Skype or Facetime, lessons are $50 for an hour, and $30 for 30 minutes. If the student would prefer to take lessons at one of my studio locations, please contact the studio at which they're interested in learning, as rates vary based on location.

Q: What & where are the aforementioned studio locations at which you claim to teach?

A: Currently, I teach music lessons at the Music & Arts located in Springfield, Virginia, and at the newly-renovated Guitar Center located in Fairfax, Virginia. I also teach private lessons in students' homes through a company called B&B Music Lessons.

Q: Are you open to working with students who have special needs?

A: Absolutely.
